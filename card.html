Queen Bee

She is the largest bee in the honey bee colony. She is fed with royal jelly only.

  • Scientific Name: Apis mellifera
  • Average Length: 18 mm
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Weight: 150-300mg

The honey bee queen mates at an early age and usually attends only one mating flight, because her sperm reserves allow her to lay millions of eggs throughout her lifetime. She can lay up to 2,000 eggs a day during active seasons.
